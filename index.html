FAQ

Common questions.

What curriculum does Don't Be Late! use? +
Don't Be Late! is fully aligned to the US Common Core curriculum. Every math question in the game maps directly to Common Core standards, so students are always practicing what they're learning in class.
What devices does it run on? +
Don't Be Late! runs on Chromebook, Windows, Mac, iOS, and Android — so it works across virtually any device your students already use at school or at home.
Is it free for schools? +
Yes! We offer a full one-month free pilot for schools — no credit card required. You'll get complete access to the game, the teacher dashboard, and student tracking during the pilot period.
What grades does it currently cover? +
Right now, Don't Be Late! has a complete question bank for Grade 4, fully aligned to the Common Core standards. We're actively working on adding more grade levels — Grades 1–8 are in our roadmap and coming soon.
How does teacher account setup work? +
Getting started is simple. After requesting a pilot, you'll receive a teacher account setup link. From there you can create your class, invite students, and start assigning levels within minutes. Our onboarding guide walks you through every step, and our team is available to help.
